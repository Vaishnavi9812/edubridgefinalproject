Donation Process

The blood donation process from the time you arrive center until the time you leave.The donation itself is about 10-12 minutes on average.

1 - Registration

*We'll sign you in and go over with basic eligibility.

*You'll be asked to show ID,such as voting card,driver's license.

*You'll read some information about donating blood.

2 - Health CheckUp

*We'll check your temperature, pulse, blood pressure and hemoglobin level.

*You'll answer a few questions about your health history

3 - Donation

*If you’re donating whole blood, we’ll cleanse an area on your arm and insert a brand new sterile needle for the blood draw.

*When approximately a pint of whole blood has been collected, the donation is complete and a staff person will place a bandage on your arm.

4 - Save Life

*After donating blood, you’ll have a snack and something to drink in the refreshment area.

*Enjoy the feeling of accomplishment knowing you are helping to save lives.
